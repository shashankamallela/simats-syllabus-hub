CSE Syllabus

1st Semester

Programming in C

  • Variables, Data types
    • Explain the different data types in C. (10 Marks)
    • Describe how variables are declared and initialized. (10 Marks)
  • Control Structures
    • Describe if-else and switch-case structures in C. (10 Marks)
    • Explain looping with examples. (10 Marks)
  • Functions and Arrays
    • What is a function? Explain parameter passing. (10 Marks)
    • How are arrays declared and used in C? (10 Marks)

Engineering Mathematics

  • Limits and Continuity
    • Define limits and continuity with examples. (10 Marks)
    • Explain the significance of limits in engineering calculations. (10 Marks)
  • Differentiation
    • State and explain differentiation rules. (10 Marks)
    • Apply differentiation to find maxima and minima. (10 Marks)
  • Integration
    • Explain definite and indefinite integrals. (10 Marks)
    • Evaluate integrals related to engineering problems. (10 Marks)
